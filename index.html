Poniary is a tool I created to make the managing of Original Character Details easier to do.

I tend to come up with a lot of ideas, and that's led to me creating over 50 Unique Characters! Seeing as I have... well, not the best memory, keeping the details of characters I use less frequently consistant can be difficult, so to combat this problem, I created Poniary.

It's also good for keeping detailed references you can share with friends!
Just share your Save File with them and direct them to Poniary!

Fun Fact: Poniary is a Portmanteau of Pony and Bestiary.
